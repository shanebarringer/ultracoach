# UltraCoach Value Proposition Canvas

## Overview

The Value Proposition Canvas helps us understand the fit between what our customers need and what UltraCoach offers. This document covers both primary personas: Professional Ultra Coaches and Serious Ultra Athletes.

---

## Value Proposition Canvas: Ultra Coach

### Customer Profile (Coach Marcus)

#### Customer Jobs

**Functional Jobs:**

1. Create personalized training plans for each athlete
2. Monitor athlete workout completion and compliance
3. Adjust plans based on performance data and feedback
4. Communicate with athletes about training
5. Track athlete progress toward race goals
6. Manage business operations (payments, scheduling)

**Social Jobs:**

1. Build reputation as an effective ultra coach
2. Maintain relationships with athletes
3. Network with other coaches and race directors
4. Contribute to the ultra running community

**Emotional Jobs:**

1. Feel confident in coaching methodology
2. Experience satisfaction when athletes succeed
3. Avoid stress from disorganized systems
4. Feel proud of building a sustainable business

#### Pains

| Pain                                   | Severity | Current Workaround                    |
| -------------------------------------- | -------- | ------------------------------------- |
| Using 4-5 disconnected tools           | High     | Manual integration, context switching |
| TrainingPeaks is cycling-focused       | High     | Adapt cycling tools for ultra         |
| Too much admin, not enough coaching    | High     | Hire assistant or limit roster        |
| Athletes confused by complex analytics | Medium   | Simplify or ignore analytics          |
| No ultra-native periodization          | Medium   | Build custom spreadsheets             |
| High per-athlete platform fees         | Medium   | Absorb cost or pass to athlete        |
| Fragmented communication               | High     | Use multiple apps (WhatsApp, email)   |

#### Gains

| Gain                            | Importance | How Measured                 |
| ------------------------------- | ---------- | ---------------------------- |
| Save time on plan creation      | Critical   | Hours saved per week         |
| All tools in one place          | High       | Number of apps used          |
| Athletes understand their plans | High       | Athlete feedback, compliance |
| Sustainable coaching income     | High       | Revenue per hour spent       |
| Athletes achieve race goals     | Critical   | Race success rate            |
| Simple athlete onboarding       | Medium     | Time to first workout        |
| Professional reputation         | Medium     | Referrals, testimonials      |

## Fit Analysis

### Strong Fit Areas

**For Coaches:**

- Ultra-specific periodization addresses primary pain
- Integrated communication solves fragmentation
- Flat pricing supports business sustainability
- Time savings through automation

**For Athletes:**

- Race-centric planning is unique value
- Simple interface reduces overwhelm
- Coach connection in one place
- Progress visibility builds confidence

### Areas to Strengthen

**For Coaches:**

- Advanced analytics (planned for Phase 11)
- Payment processing integration (Flowglad project)
- Coach marketplace for discovery (planned)

**For Athletes:**

- Native mobile app (currently PWA)
- Offline access for trail use (planned PWA features)
- Social/community features (future consideration)

## Strategic Implications

### Product Priorities

1. **Race-centric planning** is core—never compromise
2. **Real-time communication** is key differentiator—maintain quality
3. **Simplicity** wins for athletes—resist feature bloat
4. **Device integration** is expected—Garmin is next priority

### Pricing Alignment

- Coach pricing must support business growth (flat, not per-athlete)
- Athlete pricing must be competitive with Vert.run
- Free tier essential for funnel and coach acquisition

### Marketing Focus

- Lead with ultra-specific positioning
- Emphasize coach-first (vs. AI replacement)
- Mountain Peak aesthetic as brand differentiator
- Target coaches first (they bring athletes)
